How Orchestra 8000 Works

Orchestra 8000 is an intelligent AI orchestration platform that automatically routes tasks to the optimal AI model based on your specific requirements. Our triage system analyzes each incoming task and makes real-time decisions about which model to use.

The Triage Process:

  • Task Analysis: The system receives your task and analyzes its complexity, urgency, and requirements
  • Model Selection: Based on cost, speed, and accuracy priorities, the triage engine selects the optimal AI model (IBM watsonx, Groq, or Ollama)
  • Execution: The task is routed to the selected model and processed
  • Response: Results are returned with full traceability of the decision-making process

This intelligent routing ensures you get the best balance of performance, cost, and reliability for every task.
